Bavaria 50 Cruiser “Inishbeg” Inishbeg, a Bavaria 50, the flagship of the Bavaria range. A 50-footer, Inishbeg is designed for comfort and performance. Accommodation on board is luxurious and the safety, communications and navigation technology is top of the range.

Specification includes the following:

  • Luxury overnight accommodation for up to 8.
  • Four cabins - 3 doubles, one twin, each with ample storage space.
  • Saloon with luxurious seating, radio, CD player, PC with internet connection.
  • Galley with twin sinks, fridge, freezer, gas hob and oven, toaster, electric kettle, etc. Cutlery and ware for 14 people.
  • Three heads each with toilet, shower and wash hand basin. Fourth shower on swim platform.
  • Spacious cockpit with spray hood and full cockpit enclosure, can be fully closed when weather demands.
