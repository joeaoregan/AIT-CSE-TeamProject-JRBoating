The OCTO Fly C from the Estivale range of canal boats is the largest in our fleet at 15 metres long. It is the flagship boat and despite being the longest it is easy to drive thanks to bow thrusters.

Specification includes the following:

  • Four cabins and four bathrooms at the very highest comfort standards.
  • Fully equipped with a large fridge/freezer, hob and oven, TV with satellite antenna, radio / CD / MP3 player, shore supply of 220 volts and a generator to allow 220 volts power even while cruising.
  • Rear covered terrace with table and chairs and access to the upper deck. Here there is a small dining area and outdoor kitchen in the shade underneath an awning.The second steering position and two deck matresses for sunbathing can also be found here.
  • There is a swim ladder for easy on/off access and a deck shower.
