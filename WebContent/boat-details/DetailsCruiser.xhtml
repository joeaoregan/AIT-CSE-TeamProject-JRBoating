Silver Ocean (8 Berth) Based on the all new highly acclaimed Haines 400, the Silver Ocean will lay claim to be the ultimate in Shannon cruising comfort and luxury. The flagship of the Silver Line fleet boats, the Silver Ocean boasts sophistication and style together with a host of extras offers luxury accommodation for up to 8. This superb cruiser is fitted to the highest standard and comes with 2 en-suite stateroom island berths in the forward and aft cabin, together with a separate mid cabin to port.

Specification includes the following:

  • Luxury overnight accommodation for up to 8.
  • Three cabins -2 doubles, one twin, each with ample storage space.
  • Saloon with luxurious seating, radio, CD player.
  • Galley finished in avonite work surfaces, microwave, stainless steel gas oven, hob and grill.
  • Extra large bathing platform with integral teak steps to port and starboard in galley and toilets.
