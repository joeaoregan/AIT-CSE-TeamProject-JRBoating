The Old Town Discovery 119 is a compact, single seater canoe that is agile and fun to paddle. It has been a long-time favourite in the Old Town range and similar to the others, it is stable, easy to handle and tough in construction. Use with a single paddle or double-bladed paddle as preferred.

Specification includes the following:

  • Single Seat
  • Nylon Web Seats.
  • Durable yet lightweight.
  • Vinyl gunwales.
  • Ash thwarts.
  • Comfortable carrying handles.
