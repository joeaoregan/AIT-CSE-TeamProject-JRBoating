About Us

JR Boating

Located on the banks of the river Shannon, we here at JR Boating have everything you need to make your trip down the river Shannon a luxurious and enjoyable experience. From the convenience of your home, with us today and and choose from our range of

  • Cruisers
  • Canal Barges
  • Sailing Boats
  • Canoes


Never sailed before? No problem! We have experienced Skippers available to join you for a day of your trip and help you learn everything you need to know to make your trip down the river Shannon a trip to remember. or contact us today to start your adventure with us.
